History

Race Year 2023

Our latest challenge took us to the Salt to Saint 2024 relay race, featuring over 1000 riders organized into teams of 8 or fewer. Our 5-man team had a starting time of 11:00 a.m. on September 22nd.

Preparation kicked off in April, where we committed to biking 3-4 times a week, covering around 20 miles per ride. From April to September, we dedicated ourselves to daily improvement, clocking early morning and late-night rides to acclimate to various race conditions.

On race day, our meticulous planning for each leg heightened our anticipation. As the day and night unfolded, the excitement grew. By the end, we clinched 1st place in our category, triumphing over the second-place team by a remarkable 2-hour margin. This year's Salt to Saint race will undoubtedly be etched in our memories as a remarkable and victorious journey.
